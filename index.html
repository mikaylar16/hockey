Coaches

Head Coach

Derrick Henderson

A south Pennsylvania native, Coach Henderson is heading into his 2nd year as Head Coach of the Lady Buckeyes. Derrick was 2 sport high school athlete in New Jersey before attending Bowling Green State University and playing for it's Men's Club hockey team. A former roller hockey player, Coach Henderson got his start in coaching working with goalies in Phoenix, AZ. He eventually moved back to Bowling Green and coached youth goaltenders as part of the BG Youth Hockey Travel program before moving to Columbus, OH. Prior to joining Ohio State, Derrick served as an assistant coach for several local high school teams, and as a goalie instructor for area travel hockey programs.

Assistant Coach

Emily Moersdorf

A local Columbus native, Coach Moersdorf joins the Club coaching staff for her first stint as a coach. Emily is a veteran of the CCYHA girl's hockey program and a former member of the Ohio State University club team. Coach Moersdorf has come back to OSU to coach her former team after spending the previous 4 years coaching the Columbus Blue Jackets Girls' U-19 hockey team. Several former players have made their way to college club teams. Emily is a skilled player both as a skater and goaltender and has earned her level 4 USA Hockey coach certification. Given her knowledge and experience on the ice, and her drive and motivation to grow girls hockey in Columbus, Emily is a leader that our team is glad to have back in the fold..
